About Us

Driven by compassion and built on the pedestal of hope, Parashakti aims to promote sustainable development where we as the Youth of Today envision bringing about positive changes in our environment and working towards the upliftment of underprivileged children and women. Our Team represents a bunch of purpose-driven individuals and our "Shakti" as we like to put it forth is worth A Million Miracles.

Donate

Your generous donation makes a real difference. With your support, we can continue our fight to save the environment. Every contribution, big or small, fuels our ability to help grow more oxygen makers. Donate today and help us create a brighter future.

Be A Nature Saviour

Want to make a difference? We rely on passionate volunteers to help us achieve our mission. There are many ways to get involved, from donating your time to offering your specific skills. Browse our opportunities and find the perfect way to contribute to a cause you care about.

Become A Tree Parent

Ever dreamed of leaving a lasting legacy? Become a Tree Parent! Your contribution helps plant and care for new trees, creating a greener future for generations to come. It's a simple act with a powerful impact. Join the movement and watch your forest grow!
